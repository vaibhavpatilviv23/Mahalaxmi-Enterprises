How We Work

We used to deal with our customers in traditional way till now, but that is changing in 2023. Starting our journey in digitalisatiom we are allowing our customers now to browse all the products we are already selling and the all upcoming as they become available on just one place with there prices and other necessary info.

We allow our customers to place an order on our What's App number or by calling us on given number. Plus we do allow our customers to talk with us and negotiate on rates for certain orders.

By this way we are making best use of our traditional and modern technique to deliver best experience to our old loyal customers and new ones. However, as we are just starting new journey in digital way we expect some users feedback to as it very valuable for us to further improve our service.
